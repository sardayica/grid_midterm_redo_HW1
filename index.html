✴ Favorite Songs ✴

Medieval

I love the flow and intensity of this song. It's creepy and synical from the name alone, especially amongst the other seemingly normal song titles. The lyrics are about the ebs and flows of popularity as someone in the spotlight, especially highlighting the differnces between Finneas and Billy's fame.

The 90s

Once again I adore the flow and beats of this song. It's an upbeat, retro type of sound that's fun to listen too. I equally like the lyrics, which are about timess before the internet and being a kid who grew up in the transition into modern day media. Reminicing on how simple life used to be, and how much potential and hope Finneas had for his future.

Happy Now?

The lyrics of this song start off sardonic and pessimistic, talking about how Finneas seems to have everything so why isn't he happy yet? Then it switches the pace and beat to an upbeat song. The switch up is to lighten the mood and kind of make fun of himself for complaining so much when he really does have it pretty good with his fancy car and such. It brings light to the ups and downs of his life and being famous.
